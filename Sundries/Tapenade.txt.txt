This is a quick recipe for my Black Olive Tapenade of the Apocalypse.

Ingredients:
1 Cup of pitted Kalamata olives
1/2 Cup of olive oil

Method:
1. Put the ingredients together in a small food processor (or hand blender bowl)
2. Blitz until finely chopped and fairly squishy.
3. Move to a sterilized glass jar.
4. Enjoy

Stuff:
This black olive tapenade is best stored in the fridge.
